About Me

Recently I have been getting into embedded systems and microcontrollers. Spring of 2018 I took a class over them that I really enjoyed because it tied into some hobbies of mine. I enjoy programming Arduino's for various uses around my apartment. Mostly they are used for various forms of lighting, such as a motion activated light, a wifi enabled light, and programmable LED strips.
